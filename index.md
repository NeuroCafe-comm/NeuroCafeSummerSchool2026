# Neuro Cafe Summer School 2026

Welcome to the Neuro Cafe Summer School 2026, organized by the University of Tehran Neuroscience Community.

Our mission is to create an inspiring learning environment where students, researchers, and professionals can explore modern neuroscience through lectures, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities.

## Topics

- Cognitive Neuroscience
- Neuroimaging (EEG, fMRI, MEG)
- Computational Neuroscience
- Neural Data Analysis
- Machine Learning for Neuroscience
- Brain-Computer Interfaces
- Scientific Research Methods
- Neurotechnology

## Who Should Attend?

- Undergraduate Students
- Graduate Students
- Early-Career Researchers
- Medical and Engineering Students
- Neuroscience Enthusiasts

## Why Join?

- Learn from leading neuroscience experts
- Gain practical skills through workshops
- Connect with researchers and students
- Explore current trends in neuroscience
- Receive a certificate of participation
